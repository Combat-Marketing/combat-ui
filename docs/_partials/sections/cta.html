API reference
Slot Purpose
eyebrow Small kicker line above the title.
title The call-to-action heading.
(default) Supporting copy beneath the title.
actions One or more CTA buttons. Flex-wraps automatically.
Attribute Purpose
variant="simple | split | full-bleed | sticky" Layout preset. simple is centered; split places actions in a side column at ≥ 48rem; full-bleed spans the viewport with section-scale padding; sticky pins the CTA to the bottom of the viewport.
inverse Boolean. Switches to inverse surface and text colors for high-contrast moments.
align="start | center" Horizontal alignment of content and actions. simple is centered by default.
